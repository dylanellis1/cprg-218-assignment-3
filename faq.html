Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have got answers. Here are some of the most common inquiries from our guests.

General Information

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM.

Do you offer airport transportation?

Yes! Complimentary airport shuttle service is available with all bookings. Please notify us in advance.

Is Wi-Fi included?

Yes, we provide high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the resort free of charge.

Bookings & Cancellations

Can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel up to 48 hours before your arrival date for a full refund. After that, a fee applies.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a deposit equal to one night's stay is required to confirm your reservation.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
